# Q1. Should care schedules vary by region?

**Why it matters:** decides the data model for T3's import.
- Per-region schedules → plant entries need a region dimension and the site needs a region picker.
- Single schedule → flat frontmatter, simpler import, but advice may be wrong outside the nursery's climate zone.

**Still open:** whether the nursery's customers are local enough that one climate zone covers them.
