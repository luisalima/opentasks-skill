# Code of Conduct

OpenTasks is a small project, and participation should stay practical, respectful, and focused on improving the convention.

## Expected behavior

- Be direct and respectful.
- Assume good intent while staying precise about technical concerns.
- Keep criticism about code, documentation, design, or process.
- Make space for maintainers to keep the project intentionally small.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Dismissive or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeatedly pushing out-of-scope changes after a maintainer has declined them.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and contributions that violate this code. They may also block participants from the project when needed.

If you see a problem, contact a maintainer privately when possible. If no private channel is available, open a minimal public issue asking for a private contact path and do not include sensitive details.
